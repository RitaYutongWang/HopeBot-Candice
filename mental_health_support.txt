If you feel any discomfort or emotional distress during your interaction with the mental health chatbot, please try to reach out to one of the reliable, recognized and reputable mental health support contacts below:
1.	NHS Mental Health Services:
You can access a range of mental health services through the NHS.
Email address: helpdesk@nhs.net
For urgent help, call NHS 111 or visit NHS 111 online: https://www.nhs.uk/.
2.	Anxiety UK:
A charity dedicated to supporting those suffering from anxiety.
Helpline: 03444 775 774 (Monday to Friday, 9:30am to 5:30pm)
Website: anxietyuk.org.uk
3.	SANE:
Mental health charity working to improve the quality of life for anyone affected by mental illness.
Helpline: SANEline: 0300 304 7000 (daily, 4:30pm to 10:30pm)
Website: sane.org.uk
4.	Mind Infoline:
Provides information on a range of mental health topics.
Phone: 0300 123 3393 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm)
Text: 86463
Website: mind.org.uk
